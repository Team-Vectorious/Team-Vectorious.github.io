ABOUT BUNKMATE

Hey you! Do you hate looking for housing? Do you get annoyed of the really outdated websites you are forced to use? Have you gone on craigslist or facebook only to found lack of features and basic innovation? Well, then bunkmate is perfect for you!

In comes BunkMate. Bunkmate is a housing website that stands out from the rest by offering a range of features that one might need. With Bunkmate, users can filter their search results based on a variety of factors, including location, price, and preferred living situation. Each user has a detailed profile that includes biographical information, hobbies, and pictures, making it easier to find potential roommates with similar interests and lifestyles.

Finally, each listing on Bunkmate includes an organized and readable description of the property and its amenities, so renters can easily compare and evaluate different options. With these features, Bunkmate provides a user-friendly and effective solution to the common problems associated with conventional housing websites.
